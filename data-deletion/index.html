Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: June 2026

What we store

When you sign in to leave a comment on leavewithdad.com, we store only the following information from your Google or Facebook account:

  • Your display name (shown publicly next to your comments)
  • Your profile picture URL (shown publicly next to your comments)
  • Your email address (used only to identify your account — never shown publicly)
  • The text of any comments you have posted on the site

We do not store payment information, phone numbers, or any other personal data.

How to request deletion

To have your data permanently deleted from leavewithdad.com, follow these steps:

1
Send an email to dad@leavewithdad.com Use the subject line: Delete my data
2
Include identifying information In the body of the email, include the name or email address associated with your account so we can locate your data.
3
We will confirm within 30 days We will delete your comments and account information and send a confirmation reply to the email address you contacted us from.

Note: You may also delete individual comments by signing in to the site and contacting us directly — we can remove specific comments on request.

Note: Disconnecting leavewithdad.com from your Facebook or Google account via your social account settings will prevent future logins, but does not automatically delete data we have already stored. Please email us using the steps above to complete deletion.

Questions

If you have any questions about how we handle your data, see our Privacy Policy or email dad@leavewithdad.com.
